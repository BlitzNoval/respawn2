Aim Trainer

Overview

Sharpen your accuracy and reaction time with both standard and hardcore modes. The goal is simple: hit as many targets as you can before the timer runs out.

Features

• Single player
• Ray tracing for clean hit detection
• Customizable target spawn timing
• Multiple difficulty levels with a full scoring system

Development

Built in Unity 5 URP with full support across all screen sizes. Our ray tracing and scan based system tracks player aim in real time for a smooth feel.

Respawn²

Overview

A fast paced multiplayer isometric PVP shooter where strategy and chaos collide. Players queue into intense 1v1 matches and battle across tight arenas using cover, terrain height, and a range of weapons to outplay each other across seven rounds.

Features

• Online 1v1 matchmaking
• Multiple isometric arenas
• Custom weapon system with recoil and spread
• Quick respawn and round based scoring
• Controller and keyboard support

Development

Built in Unity with a heavyweight cloud based multiplayer setup for fast reconnects and match cycles, accounts and much more. We designed all arenas, weapon systems, and VFX in house, keeping the focus on tight combat flow. The game was a big test of building clean round logic, matchmaking, and fast paced combat systems on a 1 year timeline.

Bomb Brawl

Overview

Explosive online chaos in a peer to peer multiplayer arena where the last survivor takes the win. Plan your plays and read your opponents while trying not to be the one holding the bomb when it goes off.

Features

• Unity Mirror powered multiplayer
• Isometric arenas
• Cross platform on PC and console
• Emotes for in game expression

Development

All gameplay, art, animation, and assets were created in house. Made in Unity 6 HDRP over the course of one month by a three person team plus an animator. We handled all networking, UI, and level design ourselves using Mirror and Photon for hosting.

Paddle

Overview

Paddle was our two day game jam project built around the challenge of realistic paddling physics. Players steer down a dangerous river using mouse based left and right paddling.

Features

• In house water physics system
• Custom water shader and toon shader
• Endless procedural terrain

Development

The terrain system around the player is fully procedural and required a tight technical setup that we handled from scratch. Creating convincing paddling physics tied to water movement was a tough task given the two day limit, but we hit the feel we wanted. Built in Unity URP.
